## What to include

For erasure or takedown requests, please include:

1. The full URL of the entry (`https://append.page/p/<slug>/e/<id>`).
2. The reason (a short sentence is fine; a legal citation is welcome but not required).
3. Whether you are the original poster, the subject of the post, or a third party.
4. A way to reach you for confirmation (we will reply from the contact address).

## What we publish

Every action we take in response to a request is logged publicly at `/p/<slug>/moderation` with a reason. This includes both grants and denials. We do this so refusals aren't silent either. **We do not publish the requester's identity** — only the request type (erasure / takedown / etc.) and the action taken.
