# Contact

`append.page` is operated by [Yuntian Deng (`@da03`)](https://github.com/da03) on behalf of his research group.

## Where to reach us

The single channel for everything is **[GitHub issues](https://github.com/appendpage/appendpage/issues)**. It's public, traceable, and the same place we work on the code.

- **General questions, feature requests, bug reports** — open a regular issue.
- **Erasure requests** (you want a body removed under [`privacy.md`](./privacy.md)) — open an issue with the title `erasure: <slug>/<entry-id>` and the contents below. Target response: 7 days.
- **Takedown requests** (content that violates [`terms.md`](./terms.md)) — open an issue with the title `takedown: <slug>/<entry-id>`. We will publicly log the request and our decision (granted or denied + reason) at `/p/<slug>/moderation`.
- **Abuse reports** — open an issue with `[abuse]` in the title.
- **Security disclosures** — please use [GitHub Security Advisories](https://github.com/appendpage/appendpage/security/advisories/new) (private to the maintainers) instead of a public issue.

For private contact outside of GitHub, see [the contact links on @da03's GitHub profile](https://github.com/da03).

## What to include in erasure / takedown requests

1. The full URL of the entry (`https://append.page/p/<slug>/e/<id>`).
2. The reason — a short sentence is fine; a legal citation is welcome but not required.
3. Whether you are the original poster, the subject of the post, or a third party.

## What we publish

Every action we take in response to a request is logged publicly at `/p/<slug>/moderation` with a reason. **We do not publish the requester's identity** — only the request type and the action taken.
